About

Rising Vale is a gacha Discord ARPG based around the mobile app DragonVale, utilizing the Mimu bot. While its certainly useful to have at least basic knowledge of the game, its by no means required! If you're any familiar with Pokemon ARPGs, many of these mechanics may be familiar to you with randomly rolled dragons with chances to be traited or hybrids. It follows its own still developing lore and acts more as an alternate universe, taking place on the surface under the rule of the Benevolent King Bahamut where dragons have split off from wizards to explore the world and manage their own lives. While some choose to stay, most have learned to build their own society, grand structures, and farms to live off of. All new members start off with a common dragon of their choice, a rare dragon of choice, a seasonal mystery egg, and 500 Dragoncash! Members are free to make wizards as they please without any slots

Each season has a selection of dragons that get rotated every few months (See Seasons Calendar on the front page) based on when they were released in-game, as well as seasonal traits that can be earned through seasonal prompts. We have a custom made Dragonarium to hopefully help finding the dragon you rolled easier as well as what rarity each dragon classifies as! It's still actively being worked on so information such as elements & alternate forms may be missing

Our focus is more to have casual fun with your dragons and play how you want rather than purely art mechanics. We hope to have a few daily activities for those who may not be able to draw constantly, as well as ones for those that do! This may take a bit to fully develop however so the beginning will still be mostly art based.

Getting Started

When you enter the Discord you'll be prompted to fill out an introduction! once its filled out a mod will give you the member role and reward you with your starter bundle. All items and currency are tracked and processed through the Mimu Bot while you can find your dragons in the masterlist

To open your starter bundle you can head over to #item-use and enter the command "/shop use" and Starter Bundle should show up. Select it and send. If entered correctly you'll get a message back saying something along the lines of "Welcome to the Rising Vale! You've gained 1 Common Dragon Token, 1 Rare Dragon Token, 1 Fall Mystery Egg, and 500 Dragoncash! Use /inventory to check your inventory, /balance to check your balance, and head over to #‚Å†item-usage and use /shop use to open your mystery egg!"

While an mod works on getting your dragons added to the masterlist, you can participate in a few dailies over in #daily-activities to get a bit more extra cash, seasonal currency, or potentially eggs & items! These are ways for those who may be busy and cant draw constantly to still be able to passively earn currencies.

While most event related stuff isn't quite ready, we have a few mini-prompts for you to go off of for ideas to draw your dragon in or fall themed art to earn the fall currency, Spice!

Lastly, please dont feel bad if you mess something up or need to ask questions, its okay! We're all still figuring things out and how it works
