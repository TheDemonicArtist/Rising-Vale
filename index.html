About

Rising Vale is a gacha Discord ARPG based around the mobile app DragonVale, utilizing the Mimu bot. While its certainly useful to have at least basic knowledge of the game, its by no means required! It follows its own still developing lore and acts more as an alternate universe, taking place on the surface under the rule of the Benevolent King Bahamut where dragons have split off from wizards to explore the world and manage their own lives. While some choose to stay, most have learned to build their own society, grand structures, and farms to live off of. All new members start off with a common dragon of their choice, a rare dragon of choice, and a seasonal mystery egg!

Our focus is more to have casual fun with your dragons and play how you want rather than purely art mechanics. We hope to have a few daily activities for those who may not be able to draw constantly, as well as ones for those that do! This may take a bit to fully develop however so the beginning will still be mostly art based
