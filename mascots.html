Fused/Hybrid Dragons

Not to be confused with your normal in-game hybrids, this allows for dragons with fused design elements made up of two or three different species!

This allows for:
- Any mix of colors between the species
- Any mix of patterns between
- Any mix of anatomy between
- Minor design additions that would make sense for them to have

Example

Seasonal Dragons

Dragons that have an additional color palette based on one of the four seasons. This can stack four times, one for each season.

This allows for:
- Unlocking one additional color palette based on the season its applied in's colors
- Making fur long for the winter and short for the summer
- If paired with the Enchanted trait, the color palettes can be any colors
- If paired with the Rift trait they can cycle between different element designs (such as switching between a dark & light or cold & fire element themed design)
